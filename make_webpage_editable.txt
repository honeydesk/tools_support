- Open the webpage on the Chrome browser. Click inspect, and goto console, and write the below command and execute:
   document.body.contenteditable = true

- The webpage becomes editable. 
- Edit the webpage, and just have the content, and you could paste images too. 
- Finally, copy the content, and paste on libreOffice. Convert to PDF.
- You also copy the content on google doc, and markdown, and upload on github.
